ARMOR AND WEAPONRY

ARMOR

Armor refers to the equipment and / or outfit your character is wearing.

You spawn in the game wearing the "Stranded" armor.

Stranded

While you are progressing, you will find armor schematics through loot to give to a blacksmith.

The Following Armors Are the Best for Endgame Progression:

Black Diver

Prophet's Cloak

These armors provide durability for the player while also granting high Ether regen.

WEAPONRY

There are three weapon categories in the game:

Light Weapon

Medium Weapon

Heavy Weapon

The difference between these weapon types are damage output and swing speed.

At the time of this web page's release, the best weapons in each category are the following:

Light Weapon: Skull Piercer

Medium Weapon: Curved Blade of Winds

Heavy Weapon: Skyreap Blade
