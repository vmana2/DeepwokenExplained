BOSSES

Deepwoken features many monsters you can fight for your progression. These may include a Megalodaunt or Stone Knight.

Megalodaunt

Stone Knight

However, these monsters are considered lowly when compared to the true beasts of the game.

DUKE ERISIA

The creator of the Golems, Duke Erisia is the main boss you fight against to obtain your Resonance.

He is a relatively easy boss once you get the hang of him.

PRIMADON

A boss favored by many, as he drops the most loot.

Be warned, however, as he can kill you in just a few hits.

If you do not beat him within a 16 minute timeframe, he leaves and goes back to the ocean.

DREAD SERPENT

This boss is encountered while you boat across the Void Sea.

If you do encounter them, RUN.

The Dread Serpent has the highest amount of health in the game and deals the most damage.

If you are lucky enough to beat the serpent, you will be granted an ability that allows you to deal more damage to other players.
