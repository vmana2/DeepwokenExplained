DEATH

Upon dying, your character will be sent to the underworld, otherwise known as the Depths.

The rule of thumb is that if you die in the Depths, your character will be gone forever.

This place holds a numerous amounts of monsters outside of the main city in the Depths.

A monster you want to remain as far as possible from would be the Deep Owl.

These owls have the highest damage output. You especially do not want to deal with these monsters if they are corrupted.

Is there a way I can get out of the Depths?

It is possible to leave the Depths, but at a cost.

You must beat a boss in the castle of the Depths. These bosses could range from an easy enemy to the Enforcer.

I died in the Depths. What now?

If you die in the Depths, you go to the Fragments of Self.

Here, you are able to give yourself a number of items for your next journey.

You converse with your spirit and restart your character back from level 1.
